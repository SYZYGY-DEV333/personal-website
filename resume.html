Joshua Sobel
I am a musician who has more than a decade of experience as an organist, and more than 15 years of experience as a pianist and choral singer. I have a diverse repertoire spanning from the renaissance through modern eras, with specific interests in Bach, French Romantic and Impressionist music, and the art of improvisation. Since 2020, I have been pursuing a degree in organ performance from the University of North Carolina School of the Arts. Apart from music performance, I have experience as a classical recording engineer, and as an amateur computer programmer.
